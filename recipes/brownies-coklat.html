Brownies Coklat

Description

Brownies a sweetest chocolate cake ever.

Ingredients

  • 125 grams of blue triangle flour.
  • 125 grams of steamed cassava and puree.
  • 170 ml vegetable oil.
  • 6 chicken eggs.
  • 1 tsp baking powder.
  • 2 tbsp milk powder.
  • 1 tsp SP

Steps

  1. Beat sugar.
  2. SP with eggs until fluffy Mix flour.
  3. baking powder and milk powder Then sift and set aside Add flour little by little.
  4. mix well Add mashed purple sweet potato Add vegetable oil and mix well Prepare a brownie pan lined with parchment paper on top.
